In this project I am building a fully custom compass that always points at the nearest Costco. I will be using esp c3 with gps and compass sensors to geo locate where i am and have it figure out where the nearest costco is and what direction it is and real time. It will use a micro stepper motor along with esp32c3 and compass and gps sensors to make all this work, I am also working on a UI that when you plug the compass into your computer you can easily change the location it points towards to anything you want.
